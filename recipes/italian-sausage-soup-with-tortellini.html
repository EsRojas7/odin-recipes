Italian sausage soup with tortellini

Credit to Meredith Food Studios for all images used

Description

All the ingredients, italian sausage, and tortellini come together in this hearty, comforting soup.

You can also added canned tomatoes or fresh spinach with satisfying results.

Guaranteed you'll love this recipe, or you get your time back.

Ingredients to Note:

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage, remove the casing
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 cups beef broth
  • 4 large tomatoes, peeled, seeded and chopped
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1/2 teaspon dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 cups sliced zucchini
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 8 ounces fresh tortellini pasta

Process of elaboration

  1. Cook sausage in a 5 quart oven over medium-high heat until browned and crumbly,
    2. 8 to 10 minutes. Use a slotted spoon to transfer sausage to a paper towel lined plate. Drain grease. reserving 1 tablespoon drippings in the pot.

  2. Saut√© onions and garlic in drippings over medium heat until onions are translucent, 3 to 4 minutes.

  3. Stir in beef broth, tomatoes, tomato sauce, carrots, water,wine, basil, oregano and sausage. Bring to a boil.
    4. Reduce heat; simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.

  4. Skim fat from soup. Stir in zucchini and parsley. Simmer covered for 30 minutes.

  5. Add tortellini during the last 10 minutes.

  6. Serve hot and enjoy
