Italian sausage, peppers and onions

Description

This is probably the easiest recipe on this recipe website. But don't be fooled.

This italian sausage dish packs a punch of flavor with the seasoned sausage, the peppers, and the onions.

It'll surely fill your italian food craving.

Ingredients to Note:

  • Italian sausage: Sweet italian sausage is perfect for this dish, but if you like hot try using hot italian sausage.
  • Butter: Two tablespoons of butter is all you need to saut√© the veggies. You can also use Olive oil if preferred.
  • Onions: Yellow onion or red onion, if you prefer mild flavor just use half and half. Personal recommendation? Always use red.
  • Garlic: Mince four cloves of fresh garlic to add to the vegetable mix for authentic italian flavor.
  • Bell Peppers: Slice one red pepper and one green bell pepper.
  • Spices and seasonings: This sausage and peppers recipe is simply seasoned with dried basil and dried oregano.
    • although red crushed peppers could be a great addition if preferred.
  • Wine: Opt for a dry white wine such as pinot grigio or sauvignon blanc. Replace with chicken broth or beer if you don't have wine.

Process of elaboration

  1. Cook sausage in a large skillet over medium heat until brown on all sides, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from skillet and slice.

  2. Melt butter in the same skillet. Stir in onions and garlic, and cook 2 to 3 minutes. Mix in bell peppers, season with basil,
    3. and oregano and stir in 1/4 cup wine. Continue to cook and stir until pepper and onions are tender, 5 to 7 minutes.

  3. Return sausages to the skillet. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 15 minutes, or until sausage is heated through,
    4. adding more wine if needed.

  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
